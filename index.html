Contract

One promise, written clear-headed — for when you're not.

Hard rules

Ground rules are permanent. Wake-up time can only move earlier. Added rules can be removed after a 24 h cooling-off. Adding a rule never cuts existing streaks.

Bonus habits

Extras, not rules — skipping never cuts a streak. A clean day with every habit done becomes a Power Day ⚡ (gold in history, own counter). Removable anytime.

Goals

Max 3 active. A goal that runs out of slack becomes that day's hard rule.

Holiday break

Declared in advance, fixed length. Streaks pause — no growth, no cuts. Can end early, can never be extended or backdated.

Reminders

Reminders fire as in-app notifications (via service worker) whenever the app is open. For always-on push while the screen is locked, enable ntfy or Cloudflare push below.
